Why Freshgrass?

We are purpose led:

We’re consultants who live to ‘enable courageous choices.’

We are enablers:

Our greatest satisfaction comes when we give others the capacity to pursue and achieve personally meaningful goals in the face of uncertainty.

We are motivators:

We understand the importance of mindset and have proven methods to support your needs.

How we help your business thrive.

Naturally motivated we thrive working with talented people to solve complex problems. We understand ambition, uncertainty, risk and ultimately change is uncomfortable and often moving too fast can create vertigo.

This is why we focus on creating safe environments, often by making numbers visible, clear and understood from which our clients can freely make choices without having to react out of fear or based on natural instinct and gut feel alone.

We know your needs are bespoke to you and we are infinitely curious in building our understanding of what success is for you, your people, your customers and ultimately your business.

Our tools draw from the standard CFO toolbox as well as from a range of globally sourced business growth techniques:

  • Continuous Improvement
  • OKRs
  • Pricing Power
  • Sales and Negotiation
  • Zero Based Budgeting
  • FMCG costing and Activity Based Costing
  • Best of Cloud Accounting and Technology
  • KPI mastery
  • Full Potential Coaching, and Game Changing Leadership

Ultimately, it is the diversity of thought and experience that gives our consulting CFOs and Business Partners the drive, passion and ability to make a difference everyday.

Our values:

  • Curiosity, listening and learning
  • Manakitanga and respect
  • Whanaungatanga and team work
